ABOUT ME

G'day! I'm an Aussie/Italian product designer living in London and currently working at Onfido. I'm a completely self-taught designer and life-long learner who loves designing usable and beautiful products which hopefully simplify peoples lives.

I've been doing this design thing for over fourteen years now so you know I'll bring design fluency, layout, typography, and colour skills, combined with knowledge (and passable skill) of HTML5, CSS3, and javascript to all my projects.

Outside of design I'm a keen gamer (love a good RPG or story-driven game), an avid book reader (I'm really into human-behaviour and history currently), and you'll find me and my girlfriend regularly travelling — backpacking in Jordan, ballooning in Turkey, and eating delicious gnocchi in Italy (which was like eating my Nona's again!) are highlights.
